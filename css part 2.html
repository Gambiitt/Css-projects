Max Verstapen F1 World Drivers Champion
Max is currently the youngest driver to drive a formula one car, at age 16 he got his first drive with torro rosso the redbull f1 junior team. Later in 2021 in the last lap of the last race of the season Max overtook 7 time world champion Lewis Hamilton, for the lead of the race, winning his first f1 world championship.
